# Topics Navigation Update

- Added a right-side Topics panel to the chapter reader.
- Notes list every note heading.
- Q&A lists every question.
- Selecting an item scrolls smoothly to the matching content and highlights it.
- The active topic updates while the reader scrolls.
- On narrower screens, the Topics panel appears above the content and can be expanded or collapsed.
- The panel is excluded from printing and generated PDFs.
